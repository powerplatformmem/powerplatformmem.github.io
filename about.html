About the group

The Memphis Xamarin User Group meets the second Tuesday of every month from 6-8PM at the Microsoft offices located at 6750 Poplar Ave. (Forum I)
Microsoft is on the 8th floor in Suite 800. Sign in with the receptionist in the lobby then turn to the right and go to the conference room at the end of the hall named 'Beale Street'.
